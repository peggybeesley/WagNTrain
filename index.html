About Wag-N-Train

Wag-N-Train Pet Sitting was founded by JaNae Jackman in 2015. Wag-N-Tails was an idea JaNae had while she was in 6th grade to start a dog walking business. Since then, this idea has evolved and grown strong! At Wag-N-Tails, our goal is to provide caring, competent, and competitive care for all of your pet and house sitting needs. Please contact us for more information!

About JaNae

JaNae's Biography

JaNae has been a life long animal lover and rescuer. She has been a pet sitter for over 10 years in the community of Rio Verde Arizona. She has specific expertise in special needs dogs/cats/exotic animals and has extensive experience with horses. She is the co-founder of Donkey Holler Ranch. You will often find JaNae with her sidekick Deacon (her son) who is equally gifted with both animals and humans.
